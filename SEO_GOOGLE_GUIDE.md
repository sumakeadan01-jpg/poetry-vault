# Poetry Vault - Google SEO & Discovery Guide

## üîç **Getting Your Website on Google**

### **Step 1: Google Search Console Setup**
1. **Go to**: [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. **Add Property**: Enter your Render URL (e.g., `https://poetry-vault-xyz.onrender.com`)
3. **Verify Ownership**: Choose verification method:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - **HTML tag** (add to your templates)
   - **Domain name provider** (if you have custom domain)

### **Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap**
1. **In Search Console**: Go to "Sitemaps" section
2. **Add sitemap URL**: `https://your-site.onrender.com/sitemap.xml`
3. **Submit**: Google will start crawling your pages

### **Step 3: Request Indexing**
1. **URL Inspection Tool**: Test individual pages
2. **Request Indexing**: For important pages like homepage
3. **Monitor**: Check "Coverage" report for indexing status
